EVA LI

Eva Li is a graphic and UX designer, currently interning at SAP. She is also a student at SFU, in the school of Interactive Art & Technology (SIAT). Her interests are in the field of UX/UI, web and print design, and interaction design. When Eva is not designing up a storm, she's busy singing the hottest Disney and K-Pop songs at Karaoke.
